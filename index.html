About Me

Scott Fero is a freshman at Miami University majoring in Games and Simulation. He has a passion for all things storytelling and his hope is that one day he will be able to create yhis own video games, from start to finish, that tell powerful, compelling, and impactful stories. Scott Fero is from Kettiering Ohio, having done work in graphic design, video editing, and story writing. He likes to write stories in his free time and read on occasion, but most of all he enjoys playing narrative-based video games. He currently participates in Minecraft "Lore Servers" to tell stories with his friends, but he hopes to soon start what he calls a "Story Studio". As he describes it, this studio will be a collection of friends working together to tell stories with important meanings in various forms of media. He currently plans for the studio to showcase written projects, film projects, and hopefully games further down the line.
